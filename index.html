Welcome! This website was created to ensure the safety of all during a fire crisis in your area.


Instruction: To use our website simply enter a zip code in the search bar below and you will be able to see all fires in the area on Google Maps. Either click your mouse over the affected area on Google Maps or select one of the fires under “Fires in the Area” section. By selecting one the map Google Map UI will atomically the effected area and will proceed to provide you with all of the information you need to know.
